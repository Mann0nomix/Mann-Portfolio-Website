About EJ

From software auditor to virtual reality game developer, EJ has come a long way. During his undergraduate career at Lehigh University, EJ worked hard auditing software for Lehigh's Center for Advanced Materials and Nanotechnology through the school's work study program. After completing his Bachelor's degree in Cognitive Science, he earned his Master's degree in Linguistics and Cognitive Science at the University of Delaware. He then went on to develop AAA Northampton County's public website as an intern.

EJ moved on to designing POS touch screen menus for bars and restaurants at Harbortouch America. And soon after, he was blessed with an opportunity to begin doing iOS development. EJ developed 10 example iPad and iPhone apps during his training which opened the doorway to Android development.

EJ has worked with big name companies like Ecotech Marine and Highmark Health, and was a technical R&D specialist for Comcast, the largest corporation in Center City Philadelphia, developing virtual and mixed reality experiences for 6 years.

Today, EJ specializes in cloud development and support for a team of financial analysts at one of only 12 central banks in the United States, contributing to the US government's monetary policy.

In his free time, EJ immerses himself in his musical efforts, DJing for video game communities and making his own music. He is an avid gamer with a love of all things Final Fantasy. In addition, he has earned his black sash practicing Wing Chun kung fu and is now a teacher at Philadelphia Wing Chun.

EJ's current mission is to widen his skill set and expand his abilities. He has his eyes set on promoting Scrum, recruiting, and becoming an icon in tech and games.
